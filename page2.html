The Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Picture Name Description
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Full name: Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • The main character in this novel.
  • His family is the wealthy owner of San Diego.
  • For 7 years, he studied abroad in Europe, and he plans to build a school after coming back to the Philipines.
  • He desires reforms to place the Philippines in better conditions.
Maria Clara
  • Another main character and the love interest of Ibarra.
  • Her father is one the most influential figures in Binondo, and so is the man who raised her.
  • She is noted for being the ideal woman -- godly, kind, and beautiful.
Elias
  • A boater who's also seen as a criminal fugitive.
  • He desires to form a revolution against the Spaniards.
Kapitan Tiago
  • Full name: Santiago de los Santos
  • A rich, if not aristocratic, man who loves extravangance.
  • He befriends priests and collects statues of saints while believing it will get him to heaven.
  • He is the man who raised Maria Clara.
Padre Damaso
  • A strict and corrupt friar who won't hesistate to physically punish others.
  • He considered Don Rafael as an enemy.
Padre Salvi
  • A friar who values money over everything.
  • He always has a dispute with Alperes.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • The deceased father of Crisostimo Ibarra.
  • He fought for justice and rightousness.
Pilosopo Tasyo
  • Also known as: Don Anastacio
  • People either view him as crazy or wise.
  • However, he truly does have an intellectual mind and insight regard.
Sisa
  • Also known as: Narcisa
  • The mother of Crispin and Basilio.
  • She is very loving and kind to the point of being taken advantage of.
Crispin
  • A boy studying to be a caretaker of the church.
  • He is the younger brother of Basilio.
Basilio
  • Another boy studying to be a caretaker of the church.
  • He is the older brother of Crispin.
Donya Vitorina de Espadaña
  • A Filipino woman who'd rather be Spanish.
  • She always dresses and acts like them, and she married a Spaniard for his lastname.
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • A man who came to the Philippines, but didn't want to work his way up.
  • He pretends to be a doctor to get money.
Donya Consolacion
  • A Filipino woman who speaks Spanish to look superior.
  • She abuses Sisa as a way to vent her anger.
Alperes
  • A strict man in the Civil Guard.
  • He uses his men to beat up Friar Salvi's men.
Tiya Isabel
  • A cousin of Kapitan Tiago.
  • She raised Maria Clara as her mother was dead.
