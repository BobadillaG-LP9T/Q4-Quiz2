Must-see Destinations in the Philippines

The Philipines is home to various eye-catching destinations that have attracted tourists from other parts of the Philippines as well as the world. Here are three of many examples that are worth checking out:


  • Luneta Park
Located in Manila, Luneta or Rizal Park is home to the Rizal Monument to commemorate the hero's sacrifice. It's also surounded by a few national museums that you may visit to learn about the country.

  • Mount Mayon
Mt. Mayon (from magayon or beautiful) is an active volcano in Albay, Bicol, and its symmetrical form is certainly pleasing to the eyes of many. The legend of its creation is one about tragic star-crossed lovers.

  • Calle Crislogo
A town in Vigan, Ilocos wherein architecture from the Spanish colonial era is preserved. The presence of calesas and old houses give the feeling that you've traveled through time.
